Stream Notifier

This add-on displays a notification when a channel on justin.tv resp. twitch.tv goes live. Which channels are checked for being live is fully customizable.

You have upgraded Justin.tv Stream Notifier to the latest version!

Wait, don't just close this tab. If you don't want to see anymore tab when the add-on updates, just uncheck the "Open a tab with update info after an update" setting. This is hopefully the last release before the pretty much completely rewritten 3.0 version. I kind of had to release a new version as Justin.tv is no more. I thus removed the justin.tv from the name, also with foresight to the 3.0 version which will allow me to support any streaming provider without big issues. Your manually entered Justin.tv preferences will be migrated to twitch. If the migration didn't quite work, just disable and reenable the extension and everything should be fine. Bare in mind that not all justin.tv channels will (or can) migrate to twitch. I will add support for other platforms like YouTube, UStream, Livestream or Hitbox in future updates after 3.0, but hopefully before the end of the year.

Changes in 2.9:

  • Renamed to Stream Notifier
  • Removed Justin.tv and migrated existing settings to twitch
  • Better panel, but also wider. Feedback appreciated!
  • Fixed paginations: now the extension fetches more than 100 channels and can display more than 25 channels as live.
