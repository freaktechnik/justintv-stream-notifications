Justin.tv Stream Notifications

This add-on displays a notification when a channel on justin.tv resp. twitch.tv goes live. Which channels are checked for being live is fully customizable.

Get started

If you saved your login for justin.tv or twitch, you don't need to do anything. The add-on will fetch the channels you are following.

You don't have an account for either of those pages? No problem, you can also enter a list of channels the add-on should check in the settings.

On the settings page you can enter the usernames of channels you want the add-on to check or the username of someone you want to know the status of his favorites.

In all textfields you can enter multiple usernames by separating them with a comma (","). You shouldn't put any spaces.

How do I go to the settings?

Open the add-ons manager in firefox. to open it, click on the menu button and then "Add-ons" or click on this link. In the add-ons manager you click on the "options" button for "Justin.tv Stream Notifications". Congratulations! You are on the settings page!
